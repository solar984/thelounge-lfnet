Welcome to LFnet

Persistent web-based IRC chat

Web based chat is provided at https://webchat.lfnet.org/irc using the The Lounge IRC client.

You register and log in and the client will stay running even when you close your browser. You can connect from multiple browsers/devices at the same time.
